Synthetic yet Privacy-Preserving Data Generation
Replace Your Data with Anonymized
TDspora replaces your real production data with semantically correct anonymized one leveraging a state-of-the-art mathematical algorithm (differential privacy (DP). Government Agencies and Enterprises, including FAANG, employ the algorithm to protect datasets they release to the public.
Choose Size of Data Set
With a privacy budget, you choose whether you use smaller (stricter privacy) or larger (higher utility) datasets achieving the right balance between privacy and utility.
Get Secured Perimeter Premises
TDspora users don’t need access to production data and that’s why the production data never leaves the secured perimeter premises.

Fault-Tolerant Data Replication and Advanced Sub-setting
Produce Representative Samples
Training of data generation models requires representative samples extracted from the highly repetitive production data. TDspora provides an advanced sub-setting algorithm that walks through relationships between tables and extracts dependent business entities.
Design Data Set
You can tailor the representative sample and make the replica as simple as a random sample or as complex as a subset of tables with filters and relationships:
  • Choose which columns, tables, and relationships to send to the target dataset;
  • Create tables and relationships in the target database;
  • Filter tables in the sub-set;
  • Manually define relationships.
